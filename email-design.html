How I Design Emails

Every email follows a performance-first framework designed to maximize clicks, conversions, and revenue.

01

Angle First

Lead with one banger benefit. If it reads like a product description, scrap it.

02

Hero + CTA

Product-specific hero, bold headline, CTA above the fold. 2 sentences then button.

03

Visual Proof

Infographics over text blocks. Make benefits visible, not readable.

04

CTA Everywhere

Repeat CTA after every section. End with lifestyle imagery + product + final button.
